👋 Our story

Built for people who
deserve better jobs

CvCraft was born from a simple frustration — great candidates losing out because of a poorly formatted CV. We're here to fix that.

Our Mission

Level the playing field, one CV at a time

The hiring process is broken in a very specific way: brilliant people are filtered out before a human ever reads their name, because their CV didn't tick the right ATS boxes or didn't look the part.

CvCraft gives every job seeker the same polished, recruiter-ready presentation that used to require an expensive career coach or hours of frustrating reformatting.

ATS-ready every time

Every template is built to clear automated screening software — the first gate most CVs never pass.

No design skills needed

Recruiters judge CVs in seconds. We handle the formatting so your content gets the attention it deserves.

Minutes, not hours

Upload your existing CV or start fresh — either way you'll have a polished result faster than you'd expect.

What we believe in

The principles that shape every decision we make, from product features to how we support our users.

Access for everyone

Career resources shouldn't be gated behind expensive coaching or design software. CvCraft is for every job seeker, at every level.

Privacy by default

Your career data is yours. We never sell your information, and uploaded CVs are handled with strict security controls.

Speed matters

Opportunities don't wait. We obsess over making every step fast, so you can apply the moment a great role opens up.

Honest product

We don't over-promise. CvCraft helps you present yourself well — the talent and the hard work are entirely yours.

Global by design

Job markets differ. Our templates and guidance are shaped for recruiters across regions and industries, not just one market.

Always improving

Hiring trends shift and so do we. Every template, every feature, and every piece of advice is continuously refined based on what actually gets people hired.

How we got here

From a weekend frustration to a platform used by thousands.

2022

The frustration that started it

Adam, after five years in recruitment, watches a brilliant candidate get auto-rejected because their CV used a table layout. He starts building a tool over a long weekend.

2023

First 1,000 users

A quiet beta launch. Word spreads through job-seeker communities. The team grows to four and the first proper ATS-optimised templates ship.

2024

Upload & enhance launches

The ability to upload an existing CV and instantly clean, reformat, and improve it becomes CvCraft's most-used feature within weeks of launch.

Today

10,000+ CVs created and counting

Thousands of users have used CvCraft to land interviews and jobs worldwide. We're still a small team — still just as obsessed with getting the details right.
